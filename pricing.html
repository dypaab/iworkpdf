Frequently asked questions

Will iWorkPDF always be free?

Yes. The 14 tools running locally in your browser will always be free. We may introduce premium features in the future (API access, batch processing) but the core tools will never be paywalled.

Why is the Pro plan also free?

iWorkPDF is a showcase product by Yendyx. We're building trust and reputation, not maximizing revenue. The cloud features cost us very little thanks to Supabase's free tier and auto-deletion after 48h.

Do I need a credit card to create an account?

No. Just an email address. No credit card, no trial period, no automatic billing.

What happens to my files after 48h?

Cloud files are automatically deleted after 48 hours by a Supabase Edge Function. No manual action needed. This is by design — we don't want to store your files longer than necessary.
