Privacy Policy

📋 Summary

iWorkPDF is a free PDF tool built with privacy as its core principle. We don't collect, store, or share your files or personal data during normal use.

🖥 What happens to your files?

OperationWhere it runsData sent?
Merge, Split, Rotate…Your browserNever
Compress, Watermark…Your browserNever
Sign, Crop, Repair…Your browserNever
Cloud share (48h) ★Supabase storageOnly if you choose

★ Cloud sharing is entirely optional. Files are encrypted in transit, accessible only via a signed URL, and automatically deleted after 48 hours.

📊 Data we collect

Data typeCollected?Why
File contentNever—

IP addressNever stored—
CookiesNone—
AnalyticsAnonymous onlyAggregate usage, no personal data
Email (account)Only if you registerAuthentication only

🇪🇺 GDPR Compliance

iWorkPDF is designed to be GDPR-compliant by default:

  • No personal data collected during normal use
  • Account data (email) can be deleted at any time
  • Cloud files are automatically deleted after 48 hours
  • No third-party advertising or tracking
  • Data stored in Supabase EU region

🔐 Sign in with Google

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive only your email address and name to create your account. We never access your Google files, contacts, or any other data. You can revoke access at any time from your Google account settings.

🔧 Third-party services

Supabase — Authentication and optional cloud storage. Privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy
Google Fonts — Typography (Space Grotesk). Font files loaded from Google servers.
pdf-lib.js / PDF.js — Loaded from CDN once, runs locally in your browser.

📬 Contact

Questions about your privacy? Contact us at contact@yendyx.com
