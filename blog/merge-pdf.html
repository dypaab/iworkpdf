How to Merge PDF Files for Free — All in Your Browser

Merge multiple PDF files into one for free. Reorder pages, exclude pages, and download instantly. No upload.

Why merge PDFs?

Merging PDFs lets you combine multiple documents — contracts, invoices, reports — into a single file that's easier to share and manage.

Step-by-step guide

1

Select multiple PDFs

Drop or select several PDF files at once. iWorkPDF shows a thumbnail preview of every page from every file.

2

Reorder by drag & drop

Drag pages into the order you want. You can also exclude individual pages by clicking the × button.

3

Download the merged PDF

Click "Merge PDFs" to generate and download the combined file.

Tips

  • You can merge PDFs of different page sizes — each page keeps its original dimensions.
  • Bookmarks from the original PDFs are preserved in the merged file.
