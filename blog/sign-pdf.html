How to Sign a PDF for Free — Draw Your Signature Online

Add your handwritten signature to any PDF for free. Draw it with your mouse or finger. No upload, no account.

Why sign a PDF digitally?

Signing a PDF directly saves you from printing, signing by hand, scanning, and re-uploading. Your signature is drawn on a canvas and embedded into the PDF — no external service, no data sharing.

Step-by-step guide

1

Draw your signature

Use your mouse, trackpad, or finger (on touch devices) to draw your signature on the canvas.

2

Place it on the PDF

Choose the position where the signature will be placed (bottom-right, bottom-center, etc.).

3

Download the signed PDF

The signature is embedded into the PDF and downloaded instantly.

Tips

  • For a cleaner signature, use a stylus on a touchscreen.
  • You can clear and redraw as many times as you want before applying.

FAQ

Is this legally binding?

An image-based signature is valid for many informal documents but does not provide cryptographic proof of identity. For legally binding signatures, consider a qualified electronic signature service.

Is my signature stored?

No. Your signature exists only in your browser during the session and is never sent anywhere.
