How to Organize PDF Pages for Free

Reorder, rotate and remove the pages of a PDF — all by drag & drop, directly in your browser. Nothing is uploaded.

🗂 Use the tool — free

Why organize a PDF?

Sometimes a PDF has pages in the wrong order, a page scanned upside down, or a blank page you want gone. Instead of re-exporting from the original document, you can rearrange everything visually: put pages in the right order, rotate the ones that need it, and drop the ones you don't want — then export a clean PDF.

Local and private

Like every iWorkPDF tool, Organize runs entirely in your browser. Your file is read locally and never sent to a server, so it's safe even for confidential documents.

Step-by-step guide

1

Open your PDF

Drop your PDF into the Organize tool. You'll see a thumbnail of every page.

2

Reorder, rotate, remove

Drag pages to reorder them (or use the ‹ › arrows on touch screens). Click ↻ to rotate a page, or ✕ to remove it from the document.

3

Export your clean PDF

Click to generate the new PDF in your chosen order, with your rotations applied — downloaded instantly.

Tips

  • On a computer, drag & drop is the fastest way to reorder; on mobile, use the ‹ › arrows.
  • Need to only delete pages? The dedicated Delete Pages tool preserves bookmarks and internal links.
  • Reordering rebuilds the document, so the outline/bookmarks may not carry over — expected for a rearrange.

FAQ

Is my file uploaded?

No. Everything happens locally in your browser; your PDF never leaves your device.

Can I rotate a single page?

Yes — each page has its own ↻ rotate button, so you can fix just the pages that need it.

Is it free?

Yes, entirely free. No account required, no watermark, no limit.
